Inglourious Basterds (2009)

“Inglourious Basterds” is a bold reimagining of World War II, where Jewish-American soldiers hunt Nazis, and a cinema owner plans revenge in Nazi-occupied France. Directed by Quentin Tarantino, this film features intense dialogue, dark humor and unforgettable performances.
