Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • beef
  • tomato sauce
  • onion
  • olive oil
  • garlic

Instructions: Cook spaghetti. In a pan add onion and garlic, then beef and cook. Mix in tomato sauce. Serve over spaghetti.

Khinkali

Ingredients:

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 250ml water
  • 1 tsp salt (for dough)
  • 500g ground beef and/or pork
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Fresh cilantro or parsley (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 100ml cold water or broth (for juicy filling)

Instructions:

  1. Make the dough: Mix flour, salt, and water. Knead until smooth. Let rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Prepare the filling: In a bowl, combine ground meat, onion, garlic, herbs (if using), salt, pepper, and cold water or broth. Mix well.
  3. Form khinkali: Roll out dough and cut into circles (about 12 cm wide). Place filling in the center and pleat the edges, pinching at the top to seal.
  4. Cook: Boil salted water. Gently drop khinkali into boiling water and stir to prevent sticking. Cook for about 12 minutes.
  5. Serve: Sprinkle with black pepper and enjoy hot. Eat by holding the top knot and sucking the juice inside first!
