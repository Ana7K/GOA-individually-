Biography

Quentin Jerome Tarantino is an American filmmaker and screenwriter. His films are known for their stylized violence, sharp dialogue, and nonlinear storytelling. He was born on March 27, 1963, in Knoxville, Tennessee.

He gained fame with his debut film Reservoir Dogs (1992), followed by cult classics like Pulp Fiction (1994), Kill Bill series, and Once Upon a Time in Hollywood (2019).
